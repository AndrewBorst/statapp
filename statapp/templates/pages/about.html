The next step for this site is to provide a dashboard for registered guests who are interested in oil price movements. I focus on weekly trends using the broader market environment to find the probabilty that the current price is too low or too high.





Technical notes on the site:

  • Amazon Web Services cloud-based hosting.
  • Nginx server.
  • Django framework.
  • PostgreSQL database.
  • Allauth for authenication.
  • Sentry for logging.
  • Boostrap to style user interface for mobile devices.
