Traders are on an infinite data science path of Gathering, Analyzing, Predicting, and Learning. I focus on weekly trends using the broader market environment to find the probability of price directions (up, stable, or down). I trade USO options and my technical analysis based on multivariate regression modeling is tailored for that purpose. It has taken me about a dozen years of obsessive observing to come to my understanding of the oil markets. Since USO options are a second-order derivative of WTI, there are USO-specific items that I consider when trading these options. For example, USO is affected by the condition of contango/backwardation in the WTI futures curve. I offer a live analytics for registered users.
